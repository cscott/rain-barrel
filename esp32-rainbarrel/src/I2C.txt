I2C addresses used:

Flowmeter 1:  0x08
Flowmeter 2:  0x09
Flowmeter 3:  0x0A

LIS3DH:       0x19 (present on old magtag)

Snitch 1:     0x24
Snitch 2:     0x25

CAP1298:      0x28 (v2 touch sensor)

ADP1650:      0x30 (LCD backlight)

OLED featherwing: 0x3C (SH110x for flowtester)

ADS1115:      0x48 (raingauge level sensor)
MPR121:       0x5A (old cap sensor)
Motor Shield: 0x60 (selectable up to 0x6F)
ST7529:       *SPI* (transflective LCD)
